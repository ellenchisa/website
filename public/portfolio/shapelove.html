SHAPELove

SHAPElove is a global photo project exploring the theme of 'love' in over 200 cities around the world. These photos are curated by a collective of 250 people who are either Global Shapers or Young Global Leaders, both of which are World Economic Forum initiatives. Each city is exploring the theme of 'love' by responding to: "in our city, this is love," and "in our city, this needs love."
