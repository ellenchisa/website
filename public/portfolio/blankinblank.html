Blank in Blank

When I joined Kickstarter I wanted to do a quick project to brush up on Ruby/Rails. I also realized I'd spent a long time learning about interesting events in Seattle, and wanted to a way to not have that knowledge be lost when I moved to NYC. I'm still interested in this problem space: how do you find good/relevant events to your career when you move to a new city? This project also let me try out Twitter Bootstrap, Facebook auth, and TypeKit. You can get a response doc when you visit the page by selecting "Seattle" and "Product Management." I've been sharing that document more broadly lately in the hope that someone will find it useful.
