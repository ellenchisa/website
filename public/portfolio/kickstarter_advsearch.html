Kickstarter Advanced Search

I wanted to work at Kickstarter to build Advanced Search. It drove me insane that I couldn't find projects in "Category + Seattle." The shipped version allows users to create their own combination of filters from options including categories, sub-categories, locations, goals, and amount raised.

We wanted to build something that let people get to the queries they were interested in the most quickly. Most visitors were looking to combine a city and category with a sort order, so these were the three items we surfaced right away:

We provided the + menu to let users get to even more details.

Some of my favorite searches:

  • Projects that I have starred, but not backed. This is a great way to keep track of things you might be interested in backing, or see what happened wtih project you passed on.
  • Art projects that my friends have backed. My friends seem to be way better at finding art projects than I am. If you haven't connected Facebook yet, I really recommend that you do.
  • Food projects in Seattle. Mmmm food.
