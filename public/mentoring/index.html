  • I left Microsoft without having another job lined up.
  • I'm a trustee (and former Dean) of the Awesome Foundation.
  • I went to Olin College when it was still "risky" to go there.
  • I took a year off from Olin to co-found Alight Learning. It didn't work out.

Interested? Send me an email including:

  • Past: What's your story? How'd you get where you are?
  • Present: How'd you find me? How do you think I (in particular) can help?
  • Future: What are you looking forward to most in the next month?

If you're looking for help getting into Product Management, I'd suggest booking my time through Clarity.fm instead. I'll happily prepare for any specific questions you have, or prepare a mock PM interview. If this is cost prohibitive, feel free to email me and we can work something out.
