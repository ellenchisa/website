So what is /mentoring?

It's a distributed mentorship movmement! You can read more by going to mentoring.is, or you can dive right in on Github.

I decided to take part because I've had fantastic mentors throughout my life. I think a lot of people miss out because they're afraid to ask for help, so I wanted to make it really easy to ask me. It's also a little selfish - I love meeting interesting people, and I think inspiration and mentorship can come from everywhere! Hopefully we'll be able to learn from each other.


A few things I've done:

  • I'm a Product Manager at Kickstarter.
  • I left my last job without having another job lined up.
  • I was a Program Manager at Microsoft, so I went from a huge company to a pretty small one.
  • I'm a trustee (and former Dean) of the Awesome Foundation.
  • I went to Olin College when it was still "risky" to go there.
  • I took a year off from Olin to co-found Alight Learning. It didn't work out.
  • I've kept my blog (on and off) since 2008.
